Red Fort stands as a magnificent Mughal fortress with red sandstone walls and rich history.
India Gate is a war memorial honoring Indian soldiers, surrounded by lush lawns and fountains.
Qutub Minar towers at 73 meters, showcasing Indo-Islamic architecture from the 12th century.
Lotus Temple welcomes visitors of all faiths with its stunning flower-like Bahai architecture.
Humayun's Tomb inspired the Taj Mahal's design with its beautiful Mughal gardens and symmetry.
Akshardham Temple complex displays intricate carvings and showcases Indian culture through exhibitions.
Chandni Chowk bustles with activity, offering authentic street food and traditional bazaars.
Jama Masjid is India's largest mosque with marble domes and commanding views from minarets.
Connaught Place features colonial architecture and serves as a major commercial hub.
Lodhi Gardens provides peaceful green spaces dotted with 15th-century monuments and tombs.
